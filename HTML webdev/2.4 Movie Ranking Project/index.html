My top movies of all time

Bangarada manushya

"Bangarada Manushya" is a classic Kannada movie released in 1972,
directed by Siddalingaiah and starring Dr. Rajkumar in the lead role.
The film, known for its impactful storyline and memorable performances, portrays the life of Rajiv,
a man who leaves the city to rejuvenate his ancestral village.
The movie emphasizes rural development, self-reliance, and community welfare,
resonating deeply with audiences and becoming a cultural milestone in Kannada cinema.
Its success is attributed to its strong social message, emotional depth, and the iconic performance of Dr. Rajkumar.

Sampattige saval

"Sampattige Saval" is a 1974 Kannada action-drama film directed by A. V. Seshagiri Rao,
featuring Dr. Rajkumar and Vajramuni in pivotal roles.
The movie revolves around themes of wealth, power, and justice, showcasing the rivalry between the protagonist and the antagonist.
Known for its gripping narrative, engaging performances, and memorable music,
"Sampattige Saval" remains a notable entry in Kannada cinema, highlighting the classic good-versus-evil
storyline with a social message.

Diggajaru

"Diggajaru" is a 2001 Kannada film directed by D. Rajendra Babu,
featuring an ensemble cast led by Vishnuvardhan and Ambareesh. The film,
known for its strong emotional core and dramatic storytelling,
delves into the lives of two close friends whose bond is tested by unforeseen circumstances and personal challenges.
The performances of the lead actors, along with the film's music and direction,
received widespread acclaim, making "Diggajaru" a significant film in Kannada cinema.
It explores themes of friendship, loyalty, and the complexities of human relationships.
